Our story

Trucheck is a free tool that helps people evaluate the credibility of news articles. Our goal is to provide users with the information they need to make informed decisions about the news they consume and share. We believe that by improving media literacy and promoting trustworthy journalism, we can help combat misinformation and create a healthier information ecosystem.

Join the community

We're always looking for new ways to improve Trucheck, and we welcome your feedback and ideas. Whether you'd like to report suspicious content, suggest new features, or just share your thoughts on how we can make the tool more useful, we'd love to hear from you. Join the conversation and help us build a better future for news.
